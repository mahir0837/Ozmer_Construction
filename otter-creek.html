Otter Creek Co-operative Homes

Full-scale renovation and upgrades for a multi-unit co-operative housing community, improving both living spaces and shared facilities.

North York, Ontario   |   2025   |   Completed on schedule

Project Overview

The Otter Creek Co-operative Homes project involved a comprehensive upgrade of residential units and shared community spaces. Our team worked on both structural improvements and interior refinements to create safe, efficient, and modern living environments for residents.

The scope included exterior façade restoration, roofing, energy-efficient upgrades, community lounge enhancements, accessibility improvements, and interior refreshes. All work was carefully planned to minimize disruption to residents and deliver lasting value.

Key Deliverables

  • Exterior façade and roofing upgrades
  • Energy-efficient windows and doors
  • Accessibility enhancements
  • Interior unit refurbishments
  • Community lounge & common areas refresh

Results

  • Improved safety and comfort
  • Reduced energy costs
  • Enhanced community spaces
  • On-time and within budget
