A&W Project

Complete commercial build-out including structural, MEP coordination, brand-standard finishes, and interior/exterior signage installation.

Greater Toronto Area   |   2025   |   Delivered on schedule

Project Overview

We successfully delivered a complete build-out for the A&W project, covering both exterior and interior construction. From structural work to detailed finishes, our team ensured the project reflected the brand’s quality and standards while meeting strict timelines.

Scope included demolition and site prep, new partitions, flooring, millwork, lighting, equipment coordination, mechanical/electrical integration, exterior façade refresh, and branded signage install. All work was coordinated around strict health & safety standards and landlord requirements.

Key Deliverables

  • Full interior fit-out & finishes
  • Kitchen & service line integration
  • Brand signage & menu systems
  • Exterior façade refresh
  • MEP coordination & inspections

Results

  • On-time handover
  • Passed all inspections first time
  • Consistent with A&W brand standards
  • Reduced store downtime
